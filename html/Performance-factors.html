Performance Specifications

Electronics for the active antenna must be designed with the following performance specifications

  • The power consumption should be less than 0.25W
  • The -3dB freguency range of the active antenna should be equal to 10kHz to 30MHz
  • The electrical field strength at the antenna input should be equivalent to 0.45 Vrms m^-1
  • The inaccuracy of the gain at frequencies between 100kHz and 1MHz should be less than 20%
  • The antenna-referred quivalent noise spectral density limits can be obtained by linear interpolation of the following list
    • 10kHz 200nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
    • 100kHz 20nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
    • 1MHz 10nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
    • 100MHz 10nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
    • List within list
